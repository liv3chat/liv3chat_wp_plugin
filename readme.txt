Liv3 Chat plugin for Wordpress adds a professional and easy way to use our free live support chat service.

== Description ==

Want to engage with your website visitors in real time? Liv3 Chat is a free live chat service that allows you to connect and chat with your visitors and users in real time. Our chat service is free for attorneys, lawyers and legal websites only.  

Communicate from your desktop or mobile device anytime, anywhere. With Liv3 Chat help your website visitors find content, close a deal or answer some support questions.

With Liv3 Chat you have a fully loaded live support solution. Instant help for your on-site visitors, engage customers automatic or manually, share files, setup your personal live chat bot, real time monitoring, extended statistics, advanced analytics and fully customizable to suit your WordPress design. Choose from popup to slideup to slideout live chat designs, change color and animations, desktop and mobile buttons or just upload your own.

== Usage ==

The Liv3 Chat plugin adds a live support chat to every page of your website, including full support for woocommerce. You can exclude certain pages or just use our shortcode to add the chat where you want. Use our native desktop or mobile apps or any browser to answer chats, you do not need to be logged into at all times. Setup business hours in your liv3 chat account and we will inform you on a new potential client.

== What is Liv3 Chat for Wordpress? ==

Every website should have a live support chat it is like the modern contact form. It enables instant help and guidance for your website visitors. Liv3 Chat is perfect for legal websites - increase your sales and improve customer satisfaction.

== Requirements ==

Our Liv3 Chat plugin for Wordpress is absolutely free, however you will need an account with liv3 chat (which is free for lawyers, attorneys and legal websites).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Registration is free? =
Yes, absolutely. No credit card, no questions asked and no commitments but you must be a lawyer, attorney or legal website.

= I'm logged in what now? =
Follow the onscreen instruction in our Liv3 Chat dashboard or consult our FAQ how to use Liv3 Chat.

= Do I need to be logged in to be available for chats? =
No, setup business hours in your Liv3 Chat profile and we will inform you by Push Notifications or Emails when a customer needs your help.

= Is the chat window customizable? =
Of course, you have tons of options. From style to colors to animations all can be customized.

= Do you support different languages? =
Yes, a lot of them. Change your preferred language in your Liv3 settings/profile.

= More questions? = 
Contact us anytime through our live support chat we are here for you 24/7.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `liv3-chat` directory to Wordpress plugins directory (`/wp-content/plugins/`)
2. Activate the live support chat plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress
3. Click the `Liv3 Chat` sub menu on the left inside the settings menu.
4. Follow the instructions.
